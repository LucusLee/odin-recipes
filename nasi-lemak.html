Serving a good plate of Nasi Lemak

INGREDIENT LIST

  • 2 cups basmati rice, or other long-grain rice
  • 1 pandan leaf
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, white portion only
  • 1/2 leaf makrut lime leaf, optional
  • 1 (13.5-ounce can) coconut milk
  • Sambal, hard-boiled egg halves, sliced cucumber, crispy fried anchovies, peanuts, for garnish

STEPS TO MAKE IT

  1. Gather the ingredients

  2. Rinse the rice and drain

  3. Tie the pandan leaf into a knot.

  4. Lightly pound the lemongrass stalk

  5. Drop the pandan leaf, lemongrass, and makrut lime leaf, if using, into the pot (alternatively you can use a rice cooker). Pour in the rice followed by the coconut milk, bring to a boil, cover with a lid and cook for 20 minutes.

  6. Halfway through cooking, remove the lid and stir the rice briefly with a fork. Continue cooking until done.

  7. To serve, place a mound of coconut rice at the center of a plate. Top with a heaping tablespoon of sambal. Arrange the garnishes around the rice and serve.
